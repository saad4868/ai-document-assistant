Tesla, Inc. is an American multinational automotive and clean energy company headquartered in Austin, Texas. It designs and manufactures electric vehicles, battery energy storage systems, solar panels, and artificial intelligence products.

Tesla was founded in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. The company was named after Nikola Tesla, the famous electrical engineer and inventor.

Elon Musk joined Tesla in 2004 as an investor and later became CEO. Under Elon Musk’s leadership, Tesla became one of the most valuable car companies in the world.

Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Tesla’s major products include:
- Tesla Model S
- Tesla Model 3
- Tesla Model X
- Tesla Model Y
- Tesla Cybertruck
- Tesla Semi
- Tesla Roadster

The Tesla Model S was introduced in 2012 and became one of the most successful electric luxury sedans.

The Tesla Model 3 was introduced in 2017 as a more affordable electric car and became one of the best-selling EVs globally.

The Tesla Model X is a luxury SUV known for its Falcon Wing doors.

The Tesla Model Y is a compact SUV and one of Tesla’s most popular cars.

The Cybertruck is Tesla’s futuristic electric pickup truck made with stainless steel exoskeleton.

Tesla Semi is designed for commercial freight transportation.

The Roadster is Tesla’s high-performance sports car.

Tesla vehicles use lithium-ion batteries for power storage.

Tesla builds Gigafactories to produce batteries and vehicles at scale.

Major Gigafactory locations include:
- Nevada, USA
- Texas, USA
- Shanghai, China
- Berlin, Germany

Gigafactory Nevada focuses on battery production.

Gigafactory Texas serves as Tesla's headquarters and produces Model Y and Cybertruck.

Gigafactory Shanghai helps Tesla supply vehicles to Asia.

Gigafactory Berlin produces vehicles for Europe.

Tesla uses Autopilot, an advanced driver assistance system.

Autopilot uses:
- Cameras
- Radar (older models)
- Ultrasonic sensors
- Neural networks

Tesla’s Full Self-Driving (FSD) software is an advanced autonomous driving system.

FSD can perform:
- Lane changes
- Traffic light recognition
- Parking
- Highway driving

Tesla uses artificial intelligence for self-driving.

Neural networks are trained on millions of miles of driving data.

Tesla’s AI team works on computer vision systems.

Tesla vehicles process real-time data to understand roads, cars, and pedestrians.

Tesla uses deep learning models for object detection.

Tesla’s AI supercomputer is called Dojo.

Dojo is designed to train neural networks faster.

Tesla Energy is another major division.

- Solar Roof

Powerwall is a home battery system.

Megapack is used for grid-scale energy storage.

Solar Roof replaces traditional roofs with solar tiles.

Tesla aims to create a full sustainable ecosystem:
- Electric cars
